Network vulnerability assessment is a process that identifies, and classifies the security holes (vulnerabilities) in a computer, network, or communications infrastructure.

A Vulnerability Assessment identifies and prioritizes the information security strengths and weaknesses of your organization’s computing environment from a technical perspective and provides a plan-of-action so that you can mitigate risk of serious consequences from any exploitable components that can lead to unauthorized access. Give it a try today and discover your network vulnerabilities.
